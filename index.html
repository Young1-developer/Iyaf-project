brief history of iyaf

the idear of setting up this organisation was due to the poor standard of learning (i.e both islamic and modern knowledge ) by most of the youth in samaru communty and environs, despite the famous Ahmadu Bello University, other Federal,state and private higher institution of learning situated within zaria.Hence, the struggle for the formation of this forum can be traced to a group of a vibrant muslim youth who based in this community and had secured admission in to the University who wanted to rescure the state of poor islamic and academic standard of majority of the muslims youth in samaru and environs the first meeting of these vibrant muslims youth held on 23rd february,1997 with 10 members who came from different areas of samaru and were mostly students.the primary aim of the meeting was to exchange ,information etc. on the various matters concerning the muslims youth development. subsequentily in the following year(1998),the scope of the meeting was widened with led to the birth of islamic youth awareness forum(iyaf). The forum is non-profitable organisation, it focuses it activities mainly in two aspect or unit.

(a) Da,awah unit

(b) Education unit

(1.1) the Da,awah unit organise public lectures on current issues of national and internation interest atleast twice a year. it also conduct da,awah outreach programmes for secondary and islamiyya schools within the environs .

(1.2) Education unit organise and extra moral classes for coaching pupils of secondary schools from jss 1-ss3, the venue for these classes is Demonstration Secondary school,Ahmadu Bello Univeersity, Zaria. the classes holds on thursday and friday between 4:00pm - 6:00pm while on saturdays and sundays between 8:30am - 12:30pm presently we have a toatl number of 223 students and 44 teachers
